Installation

To install Genio, you can do so in one of two ways:

  • The Simple Method: Upload genio-wt.zip file via the the WordPress Admin Panel. To do so, go to “Appearance” > “Themes” in the menu, then select the “Install Themes” tab at the top of the page. Once you are at this page, select “Upload,” then “Choose File” to locate the zip folder on your computer. After doing so, click “Install Now.”



  • The Roll Up Your Sleeves and Use a Little Elbow Grease Method: Upload genio-wt folder to the /wp-content/themes/ directory of your WordPress installation via your chosen FTP client.

Neither method has an advantage over the other, it is simply a matter of preference.

Activation

Once you have installed the theme, you can go back to the “Themes” page in the WordPress Admin Panel (“Appearance” > “Themes”) and select “Activate” on Genio. Then you will see a notification message to activate the required plugin. Follow the following steps to install and activate this plugin.

First click on “Begin installing Plugin” link appearing on notification message.



Once you click on this link, you will be redirected to a page containing the required plugin. On Genio Core Plugin click “install”.



Then the plugin will be installed and activated. Now you can return to dashboard.

Home Page

Once you have activated the theme and the required plugin, A new menu will appear in your dashboard with sub-menu items. Navigate to these pages to insert your Projects, Categories, Skills, Resume, Hobbies, Pricing Plans, Work Process, Testimonials, Clients and Services. we’ve included notes to help you out along the way as you work your way through the options. Also you can import the demo data (Explore Demo Section.)

Setting Up Home Page

Setting up your home page is the same as setting up any other regular pages, except that you need to specify in the settings which page will be your Main Home Page.

Follow these steps to set up home page:

  • Step 1 – Go to “Pages” > “Add New” in the WordPress Admin Panel and set page title to “Home” and click “Publish”.
  • Step 2 – Also Go to “Pages” > “Add New” in the WordPress Admin Panel and set page title to “Blog” and click “Publish”. Now you have two pages, one for blog and another for home.
  • Step 3 – Navigate to Settings > Reading tab.
  • Step 4 – Select A Static Page option.
  • Step 5 – Choose the page you want as your home page from the Front Page dropdown list.
  • Step 6 – This is also the same spot you select the blog page as the Posts page.

Theme Customizer

To get to the WordPress Theme Customizer in Genio, go to “Appearance” > “Customize” in the WordPress Admin Panel. Once you are there, you will be presented with a screen consisting of the options panel on the left and the preview of your site on the right. It should be noted that nothing you change in the Theme Customizer will be committed until you select “Save & Publish.” This is an incredibly handy feature as it lets you to view everything you’re changing on your site before any of your visitors see anything you’re updating, allowing you to get things exactly the way you want them before making them live.




With theme customizer you can change:

  • Site Title & Tagline Section:
    • Site Logo: To select site logo.
    • Site Favicon: To select site favicon.
    • Display As: select whether to display site title or site logo or Hire me link in navigation menu.

  • Styles Section:
    • Home Template: to select home template from previously created templates in home builder page.
    • Skin: to select theme skin. Genio ships with 6 skins to select between.
    • Blog Page Custom Header: custom image to be used as background for blog page header.

Widgets

Genio ships with sidebar in Blog Page. It is widgets ready so let's explore how to set wordpress default widgets and genio additional widgets.

Setting Default Widgets

To Add Widgets, Follow The Steps Below.

  • Step 1 – Navigate to Appearance > Widgets to access the list of widgets and widget sections that they can be added to. The left hand side shows all the widgets you can use. The right hand side shows all the different sections you can add widgets to.
  • Step 2 – Simply drag and drop the widget you want into the widget section you want on the right hand side.
  • Step 3 – When finished, make sure to click Save in the widget editing box to save the widgets added to your sidebar.

Setting Up The Twitter Widget

To Setup The Twitter Widget, Follow The Steps Below.

  • Step 1 – First go https://dev.twitter.com/apps and sign in with your own twitter ID.
  • Step 2 – Click on the “Create A New Application” button.
  • Step 3 – Fill out the required fields. The name and description can be anything, it does not have to be a certain name. You can leave the Callback URL field empty.
  • Step 4 – After creating the app, click “Create My Access Token” button to generate the necessary codes.
  • Step 5 – Copy and paste consumer key and consumer secret into the corresponding twitter widget fields in “appearance > widgets” of the Genio theme.
  • Step 6 – Make sure you click Save, then please wait because Twitter has a 10 minute cache on their end, so changes can take that long to take effect.
  • The cache settings in your twitter widget used to reduce loading of your website as it caches your tweets for defined period.

Setting Author Box Widget

Upgrade

Genio will show notification in your wordpress dashboard if new updates released.

How to upgrade:

  • If update notification shown in your dashboard, Download latest version from themeforest.
  • Extract genio-wt.zip archive and upload extracted genio-wt folder to /wp-content/themes directory with replacing.
  • It is very important to update plugin that are included with the theme so Extract genio-wt.zip archive in genio-wt/core/plugins folder and then upload extracted genio-wt folder to /wp-content/plugins directory with replacing.
